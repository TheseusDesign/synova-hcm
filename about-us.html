About Synova Human Capital Management

With a strong commitment to service, compliance, and innovation, we aim to take the complexity out of back-office operations, giving you more time, confidence, and clarity to lead your organization forward.

At Synova Human Capital Management we have over 35 years of experience. We are dedicated to supporting small to mid-sized businesses by delivering tailored solutions in Human Resources, Payroll, and Time Keeping. Our goal is to simplify and streamline workforce management so that business owners can concentrate on what they do best running and growing their business.

We understand that no two businesses are the same. That's why we take a customized approach, designing payroll and HR strategies that align with each client's specific needs, industry, and company culture. From ensuring accurate and timely payroll processing to managing employee records, benefits administration, compliance, and time tracking, we act as a seamless extension of your team.

Let us handle the details—you focus on your business.
